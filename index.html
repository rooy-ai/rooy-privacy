ROOY Collector — 隐私政策

生效日期:2026-06-28

ROOY Collector(以下简称"本 App")是一款滑雪运动数据采集应用。本政策说明我们收集哪些数据、如何使用,以及你的权利。

1. 我们收集的数据

  • 运动传感器数据:通过蓝牙从你佩戴的 ROOY 传感器采集的高频 IMU 数据(加速度、陀螺仪、磁力计、气压/温度)。
  • 位置信息(GPS):采集期间记录的 GPS 轨迹,用于与运动数据对齐(包含在后台采集时)。
  • 设备标识:传感器序列号 / 蓝牙标识,用于区分左右脚设备并归属数据。
  • (可选)若你登录:你的手机号,用于将数据归属到你的账号。

2. 数据用途

所采集的数据仅用于:连接与读取你的传感器、记录与展示运动与轨迹、上传到我们受控的服务器进行滑雪动作分析,并向你呈现分析结果。

3. 蓝牙与位置权限

蓝牙权限仅用于连接 ROOY 传感器;位置权限仅用于记录 GPS 轨迹。我们不会将这些权限用于其它目的。

4. 数据存储与共享

数据保存在你的设备上,并上传到我们受控的后端服务器进行处理。我们不会出售你的数据,也不会与无关第三方共享;仅在为你提供分析服务所必需的范围内处理。

5. 你的权利

你可以随时停止采集、断开设备或卸载本 App。如需删除已上传的数据,请通过下方邮箱联系我们。

ROOY Collector — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-28

ROOY Collector ("the App") is a ski-motion data collection app. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data we collect

  • Motion sensor data: high-rate IMU data (accelerometer, gyroscope, magnetometer, barometer/temperature) read over Bluetooth from your ROOY sensors.
  • Location (GPS): a GPS track recorded during collection (including in the background) to align with the motion data.
  • Device identifiers: sensor serial / Bluetooth identifiers, to tell the left/right units apart and attribute data.
  • (Optional) If you sign in: your phone number, to attribute data to your account.

2. How we use it

Collected data is used only to connect to and read your sensors, record and display motion and track, upload to our controlled servers for ski-technique analysis, and present the results to you.

3. Bluetooth & Location

Bluetooth is used only to connect to ROOY sensors; Location is used only to record the GPS track. We do not use these permissions for any other purpose.

4. Storage & sharing

Data is stored on your device and uploaded to our controlled backend for processing. We do not sell your data and do not share it with unrelated third parties; it is processed only as needed to provide the analysis.

5. Your choices

You can stop collection, disconnect devices, or uninstall the App at any time. To delete uploaded data, contact us at the email below.
